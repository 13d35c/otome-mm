I recently finished Yoosung's route (and I like him very much to the point I feel like I am betraying him by playing another route) so I gained access to Extras section of the game and can see the chat time stamps. Some characters call a while after the chat ended -- about 30 minutes after I chatted with Zen in Common Route 01: Day 02 "Romance Novel Company", Yoosung called and I missed it. I will not post any conversations to avoid spoiling myself in case I lose my memories and I end up replaying this game.
